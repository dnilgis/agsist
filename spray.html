🧮 Spray Mix Calculator

Enter your carrier rate, tank size, and each product's label rate. Get exactly how much of every product and how much water goes in each tank — including the partial last tank so you don't over-mix — plus whole-field totals. Works in oz, pints, quarts, gallons, pounds, % v/v, and per-100-gallon rates.

Products

Match your label: most herbicides are per acre (fl oz / pt / qt / lb). AMS, surfactants (NIS/COC/MSO) and drift agents are usually per 100 gal or % v/v. Mixing two or more products? Run a jar test first.

Calculator only — the product label is the law. Verify rates, carrier volume, and adjuvant compatibility against the label and a jar test before mixing. Add products to the tank in the correct order (see the Tank Mix Order guide below).

🎯 Sprayer Calibration Calculator

Are you really applying the rate you think? Check your actual gallons per acre from ground speed, nozzle spacing, and measured output — or work backward to the nozzle output a target rate needs. Uses the standard broadcast formula: GPA = 5940 × GPM ÷ (MPH × spacing).

Standard broadcast calibration (constant 5,940 for inch spacing). Catch each nozzle and average them — replace any nozzle more than 10% off the mean. Always confirm against your monitor and re-calibrate when you change nozzles, pressure, or speed.
