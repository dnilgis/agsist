About Soybean Futures

Soybean futures (ZS) are traded on the Chicago Board of Trade (CBOT), part of the CME Group. Each contract represents 5,000 bushels of #2 Yellow Soybeans. Prices are quoted in cents per bushel.

Trading hours are Sunday-Friday 7:00pm - 7:45am CT and Monday-Friday 8:30am - 1:20pm CT. The most actively traded contracts are typically the front month and the November new crop contract.

Key factors affecting soybean prices: USDA reports, South American crop conditions, Chinese demand, soybean oil and meal prices, and weather during critical growing stages.
