WHAT'S THERE

  (each covers a chunk of states, e.g. roughly AL-through-KY in one
  file). Exact names/splits vary by year - grab whatever the newest
  fiscal year offers.

DO THIS - IN TWO STEPS, NOT ONE
  STEP 1 (today, 1 file): download ONE file from the NEWEST year -
    pick the smallest/last state-range file. Upload just that one to me.
    I validate the real schema (headers, program codes, payee format)
    before you burn time on the rest. Probes before pipelines.
  STEP 2 (after I confirm): download the REST of that year's files
    and upload them all. If the site offers prior years and the
    downloads are painless, the newest year alone is enough to launch -
    history can come later.

DON'T

    what the site gives you (re-saving can mangle encodings).
  - Don't rename the files - the names carry the state-range info.
