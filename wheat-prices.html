About Wheat Futures

Chicago wheat futures (ZW) are traded on the Chicago Board of Trade (CBOT). Each contract represents 5,000 bushels of soft red winter wheat. Prices are quoted in cents per bushel.

Trading hours are Sunday-Friday 7:00pm - 7:45am CT and Monday-Friday 8:30am - 1:20pm CT. Kansas City (HRW) and Minneapolis (spring wheat) futures trade on their respective exchanges.

Key factors affecting wheat prices: Global weather (especially Black Sea region), export competition, USDA reports, quality concerns, and currency fluctuations affecting export demand.
