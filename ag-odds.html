üí° How Prediction Markets Work

Prediction markets let people put real money on whether something will happen. The price of a "yes" share tells you how likely the crowd thinks an event is. If "yes" shares cost 31¬¢, the market says there's about a 31% chance it happens. These markets have been more accurate than polls and pundits for elections, policy, and economic events.
