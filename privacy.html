Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

AGSIST collects minimal information to provide our agricultural tools and market data services:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/region level).
  • Local Storage: Some calculators may store your inputs locally in your browser for convenience. This data never leaves your device.

Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone)
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across sites

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Market Data Providers: Price data is sourced from public APIs and may be delayed.

Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS. We do not store sensitive personal information on our servers.

Your Choices

  • You can disable JavaScript to prevent analytics tracking
  • You can clear your browser's local storage to remove any saved calculator inputs
  • You can use browser privacy tools or extensions to limit tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
