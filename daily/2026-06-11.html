🐂CATTLE BOUNCE HOLDS ON CLARITYMEDIUM CONVICTION
📡DRIVERNew World screwworm confirmation removes uncertainty premium
↺Cattle: bounce held, feeders lagging confirms selective recovery
Live cattle gained $2.17 to $241.78 as the **screwworm confirmation** paradoxically steadied futures by removing uncertainty. The pest is confirmed, the management protocols are known, and the discount got priced Tuesday. Feeders barely budged at $354.52, up just 35 cents, keeping the complex split that signals selective recovery. Processing capacity is the real constraint, not disease fear. The bounce is real but narrow, exactly what you'd expect when the unknown becomes known.
Confirmation removed uncertainty, processing capacity still the constraint.

🧠 THE MORE YOU KNOW
Why WASDE day always thins the crowd
Corn's open interest dropped across the board ahead of this morning's WASDE, and there's a reason: the report can move markets 3% in either direction based on a single line revision. Smart money steps back rather than guess which way the number breaks. The volume comes back after the release when the direction is clear. Today's positioning phase typically sees 15-20% lower volume than the session average, but tomorrow's follow-through gets the real conviction.
