🧠 THE MORE YOU KNOW
Why Planting Speed Actually Matters
Every day of delayed corn planting after the optimal window costs roughly 1 bushel per acre in yield potential. It's not just old wives' tales — university data consistently shows this penalty. The corn plant's genetics are programmed for specific day-length triggers, and late planting throws off the entire reproductive cycle. This is why April weather can move markets more than USDA reports during planting season.
