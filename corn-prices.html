About Corn Futures

Corn futures (ZC) are traded on the Chicago Board of Trade (CBOT), part of the CME Group. Each contract represents 5,000 bushels of #2 Yellow Corn. Prices are quoted in cents per bushel.

Trading hours are Sunday-Friday 7:00pm - 7:45am CT and Monday-Friday 8:30am - 1:20pm CT. The most actively traded contracts are typically the front month and the December new crop contract.

Key factors affecting corn prices: USDA reports (WASDE, Crop Progress), weather conditions in the Corn Belt, export demand, ethanol production, and global supply/demand dynamics.
